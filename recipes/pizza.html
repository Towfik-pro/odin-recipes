Pizza

Description: Margherita Pizza is a simple yet delicious pizza topped with fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil. Its golden, melted cheese and fragrant basil make it a favorite worldwide.

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt & pepper
