Lasagna

Description: Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish with long noodles topped with a hearty, slow-cooked meat sauce. It’s comforting, filling, and easy to make for everyday meals.

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Onion, garlic
  • Tomato sauce / crushed tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, oregano, basil
  • Grated cheese (parmesan or mozzarella)

STEPS

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water, drain.
  2. Fry onion & garlic in olive oil, add ground meat, cook.
  3. Pour in tomato sauce, season with salt, pepper, oregano, basil â†’ simmer.
  4. Mix spaghetti with the sauce or pour sauce on top.
  5. Sprinkle cheese and serve hot.
